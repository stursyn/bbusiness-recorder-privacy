# Privacy Policy for BBusiness Recorder Chrome Extension

**Last updated:** August 08, 2025

BBusiness Recorder Chrome Extension ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our Chrome extension.

## 1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of data during your use of the extension:

- Tab URLs
- Click events
- User input (such as selected elements)

We also collect the **generated automated test code**, which is sent to our **own local server** for processing and storage of test cases.

## 2. How We Use Information

We use the collected data solely for the purpose of:

- Recording user interactions
- Generating automated test scripts locally in your browser
- Sending generated test cases to a local server for storage and further use

We do **not** collect any personally identifiable information (PII) unless explicitly provided by the user.

## 3. Data Sharing

We do **not** sell, rent, or share your data with any third parties.

Generated test cases are only sent to a **self-hosted local server** managed by us and are not transmitted to any external services.

## 4. Data Storage and Retention

Data is stored either locally within your browser session or on the local server mentioned above. We do not store user data permanently unless necessary for test case collection.

## 5. User Control

You can disable or uninstall the extension at any time to stop data collection. You also have full control over your test case data.
